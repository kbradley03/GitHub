About

Picky eater is a club where vegans and other people whom are picky about the things they put in their bodies due to dietary restrictions or conscious reasons aren’t ostracized but supported. Peace, love and veggies are the mantra of the Picky Eater Club. The Picky Eater Club envisions a Chicago and even greater a planet where people can have numerous options to consume healthy, organic, earth friendly and flavorful vegan food. We embrace being different in this club! We firmly believe that by expanding thoughts and taste pallets we will be able to end the earth’s pain! Not to mention stop war, end child obesity has free parking everywhere for everyone in the city of Chicago! Picky Eater currently provides delectable vegan cupcakes in a variety of flavors. We use all organic ingredients and earth friendly packaging for our cupcakes. There will be more vegan items to come in the near future. Come join our club and members are welcome!
